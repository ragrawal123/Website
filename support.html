Tips We Can Offer

1. Do Your Research: It is extremely important to do research because these projects are hard, and even professional developers use the Internet, so make sure to look stuff up to get a better understanding of what you are doing.
2. Don't Wait Until The Last Minute: The projects take a really long time. Do not wait until the weekend before to finish it, do bits by bits of it as time goes on and you will thank yourself for doing it gradually rather than spending 8-12 hours over the weekend.
3. Work With Friends: The projects can be very draining, especially for one person to do. We recommend that you work with your friends because the projects then become fun and engaging, and you can also discuss more ideas together and code better. You don't have to work with other people, but it's a lot easier to do so.
